A — Turnbuckle + Hirth:
Angled rods meet at center eye on belly-to-screen axis. Turnbuckle sets fold angle. Hirth half-moon discs (flat pizza on back) lock butterfly. Butterfly nut clamps teeth. Magnet for quick-release.
B — Oarlock + Bolt-draw:
Central hinge rod. Oarlock eyes at each half's edge. Draw bolt runs front-to-back (Z-to-W left, M-to-O right). Rotating grip draws rod end-nut for butterfly. Continuous adjustment.
C — Gimbal:
Outer ring = fold axis, inner ring = butterfly axis. Each axis has its own thumb screw. Two separate lock operations required.
D — Ball + Cam Lever:
Ball-and-socket for both axes. Cam lever (flip to lock). Simpler but friction-based — may drift under typing force on lap.
